IoT BrewBox

September 2023 — Present

As the Instrumentation Lead of Brewing Internet of Things, my team and I have been tasked with creating a modular and open-source instrumentation device for homebrewing. Our first version, using an ESP32, was a simple proof-of-concept of what the device could be. Utilizing a Whitebox T1, an Arduino Uno, and a Raspberry Pi, the new and updated model will feature a Grafana dashboard, with data backed up to our database solution.here!

Cluck Guard

May 2023 — August 2023

My latest personal project, Cluck Guard, is a low-cost, low maintenance, and open-source alternative to other chicken doors on the market. Utilizing a pulley system, the door is lifted with a servo that rotates a pulley 360 degrees. The project is temporarily on hold at an 80% completion. here!
